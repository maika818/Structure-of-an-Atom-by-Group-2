Welcome to Structure of an Atom webpage!

Welcome to the fascinating world of Atomic Structure! At the heart of all matter lies the atom, the smallest unit of an element. Inside each atom, you'll find a central nucleus made of protons and neutrons, surrounded by a cloud of fast-moving electrons. These subatomic particles interact in ways that define the properties of the elements and the bonds they form, making atomic structure the foundation of chemistry and physics. As we explore this topic, you'll uncover how the arrangement of electrons shapes the universe, from the simplest hydrogen atom to complex molecules!
